Barefoot Bay, Est. 2006

Barefoot Bay, located in Dorros Cove on the southern end of Elbow Cay in the Abacos, Bahamas, is a beach house with 3 pods, completely renovated in 2021. It is designed for up to 16 guests featuring 6 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms. The spacious layout offers maximum privacy for a groups that would like quieter bedrooms and a separate living area. There's a large deck, a private pool, and a widow's peak all offering views of the Atlantic Ocean and the Sea of Abaco.

Amenities

Beautiful Views

Enjoy breathtaking views & gorgeous sunsets from the deck & widow's peak, featuring views of the Atlantic Ocean & the Sea of Abaco.

Great Location

The house is located on the southern end of the island, just a short distance from the idyllic Tahiti Beach.

Large Groups

The house is perfect for large families or groups. It comfortably sleeps up to 16, & has 6 bedrooms & 4 bathrooms.

Recently Renovated

The house was renovated in 2021 following Hurrican Dorian. It features brand new furniture, appliances, & deck.

Private Pool

Enjoy a refreshing swim in the private pool on the deck, featuring beautiful views of the Atlantic Ocean & Sea of Abaco.

Boat Slip Included

During your stay, you'll have access to the house's boat slip located in Dorros Cove (2 mins away from the house).

Directions

After landing in Marsh Harbour, you'll need to travel by boat to Elbow Cay. You can do this via private boat or the ferry service. G & L Ferry & The Ferry at the Crossing are some of the ferry services available.

If needed, the house caretakers will arrange to pick you up from the ferry dock & bring your group (& your luggage) to the house.
