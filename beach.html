Barefoot Beach, Est. 2003

Barefoot Beach, located in Marnie's Creek on Elbow Cay in the Abacos, Bahamas, is an oceanfront two story beach house, designed for up to 16 guests featuring six bedrooms and four bathrooms completely renovated in 2021. It is on the southern end of Elbow Cay and has its own private beach, a spacious living area with high ceilings, a wraparound porch, and a pool overlooking the ocean.
