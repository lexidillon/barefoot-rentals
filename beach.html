Barefoot Beach, Est. 2003

Barefoot Beach, located in Marnie's Creek on the southern end of Elbow Cay in the Abacos, Bahamas, is an oceanfront two story beach house, completely renovated in 2021. It is designed for up to 16 guests featuring 6 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms. It has its own private beach, a spacious living area with high ceilings, a wraparound porch, and a pool overlooking the ocean.

Amenities

Beautiful Views

Enjoy breathtaking sunrises & gorgeous sunsets from the deck, beach, & wraparound porch.

Great Location

The house is located directly on the beach. It's also just a short drive into town & to the idyllic Tahiti Beach.

Large Groups

The house is perfect for large families or groups. It comfortably sleeps up to 16, & has 6 bedrooms & 4 bathrooms.

Recently Renovated

The house was renovated in 2021 following Hurrican Dorian. It features new furniture, appliances, & deck.

Private Pool

Enjoy a refreshing swim in the private pool on the back deck, featuring beautiful views of the Atlantic Ocean.

Boat Slip Included

During your stay, you'll have access to the house's boat slip located in Marnie's Creek (2 mins away from the house).

Directions

After landing in Marsh Harbour, you'll need to travel by boat to Elbow Cay. You can do this via private boat or the ferry service. G & L Ferry & The Ferry at the Crossing are some of the ferry services available.

If needed, the house caretakers will arrange to pick you up from the ferry dock & bring your group (& your luggage) to the house.
